Masala Maggi

Vegetable Masala Maggi is not only a easy to make food but also
one of the most yummy and tangy dish loved by people of all ages
and especially by the children.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Packet Maggi Noodles
  • 1 Small Onion
  • 1/3 Cup Chopped Carrot
  • 1/3 Cup Chopped Capsicum (Green Bell Pepper)
  • 1/3 Cup Green Peas
  • 1 Large Tomatoe
  • 1 Green Chilli
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Turmeric Powder (Haldi)
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Chaat Masala
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Oil

Recipe:

  • Heat oil in a pan and add finely chopped onions to it.
  • Also add some finely chopped green chillies. Fry the onion for 2 minutes as it starts browning.
  • Add chopped carrots, capsicum and peas to the pan.
  • Cook the vegetables for 2-3 minutes.
  • Add chopped tomatoes to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes till the tomatoes become soft.
  • Then add salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder (haldi) and chaat masala.
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Turmeric Powder (Haldi),1/2 Teaspoon Red Chilli Powder,1/2 Teaspoon Chaat Masala
  • Also add 2 packets of maggi tastemaker masala.
  • Mix well and roast the spices for couple of minutes.
  • Add 2 cups of water to the pan and bring it to a boil.
  • Then add the maggi noodles to the pan and boil for 3-4 minutes.
  • Stir the noodles occasionally and cook till all the water is absorbed.
  • Vegetable masala maggi is ready. Serve it hot.
